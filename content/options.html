Subtitles will look like this.

Your preferred language of subtitles.

When the video you are watching has subtitles in your preferred language those subtitles will be shown on the video.

When not enabled you have to click on the extension to see if there are any subtitles available for the video you are watching.

This extension is only active on YouTube (www.youtube.com, m.youtube.com) and Weverse (weverse.io, m.weverse.io). It will not check for subtitles on any other website.
